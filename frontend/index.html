About Us

At Rejoice Estate Consultants, we're a team of passionate professionals with a vision for the future. While we've established ourselves in Mumbai's Western Suburbs through successful real estate investments and consultations, we believe there's a smarter way to navigate this dynamic market.

Our core investment model has delivered strong yields, but we recognize its limitations. Growth hinges heavily on capital, especially in a city like Mumbai where property prices are a significant hurdle. This inspired us to explore a more scalable path.

On the other hand, real estate services hold immense potential for growth, yet the industry remains largely unorganized and reliant on manual processes. This is precisely where we aim to disrupt the status quo.

We're pioneering the future of real estate with cutting-edge AI and Large Language Model (LLM) solutions. By integrating automation and streamlining workflows, we're committed to transforming the way business is done in Mumbai's real estate landscape.


Stay tuned as we pave the way for a more efficient, transparent, and data-driven real estate experience for all!

FOS: Fediverse On Steroids

Real Estate Reimagined with FOS i.e. Fediverse on Steroids!

More than 80% of business in India are unorganised and rely heavily on manual processes especially in the real estate sector.

Goodbye, Industrial Era! Leave behind the limitations of centralized systems. Embrace the collaborative power of community-driven business and tech solutions built for the community era !

Introducing FOS - Fediverse on steroids :

Decentralized Power:

Decentralized & Serverless: Experience a real estate ecosystem independent of centralized control, built on a serverless infrastructure for ultimate scalability. Your data, your rules!

Blockchain-powered Security: Ensure the highest level of data security and transparency for your business through blockchain technology.

Collaborative Knowledge:

Community-driven Knowledge Base: Tap into a wealth of real estate knowledge built and maintained by the community itself. Learn from theSLM/LLM by the Community: Inspired by Project Petals (https://github.com/bigscience-workshop/petals), FOS empowers the community to create and manage its own AI assistants (SLM/LLM) for a truly collaborative experience.

Seamless Workflow:

Seamless Integration: Connect your existing social media and messaging platforms with ease. No more juggling multiple platforms!

Advanced Search & Filtering: Find exactly what you're looking for with powerful search functionalities and customizable filters integrated with AI.

Empowering Users:

Real-time Collaboration: Connect and collaborate with other users in real-time for a more efficient and streamlined workflow.

Data Ownership & Control: Maintain complete control over your data within the secure decentralized network. Your data, your choice!
